Myopia Management with Hoya MiyoSmart, Essilor Stellest and Zeiss MyoCare Book Appoinment

We are excited to announce the availability of Hoya MiyoSmart, Essilor Stellest and Zeiss MyoCare lenses at Trinity Optometry Eye Clinic

MiyoSmart – A World First Our myopia management expert Optometrist Samuel Livingstone K commented: “MiyoSmart is a very exciting addition to our ever-growing toolkit for slowing the progression of myopia in children. MiyoSmart is the first spectacle lens product approved for the treatment of myopia. The levels of myopia progression reduction reported in the MiyoSmart 2-year clinical trial data are similar to those found with other methods we use in practice such as MiSight contact lenses. MiyoSmart allows us to start myopia management strategy right away in these children and we can slowly introduce the idea of contact lens wear later. MiyoSmart also offers us the opportunity for families to use both spectacles and contact lenses options in conjunction with one another to allow the child to choose when contact lenses or spectacles are more suitable to their task without having to compromise myopia progression.
Children are spending more time indoors and on screens doing schoolwork after COVID-19. The result of this increase in time spent indoors and the increased use of computers, tablets, and smartphones for both schoolwork and leisure is that we are seeing an acceleration of myopia in many children. If we can achieve 100% of a child’s day with a myopia management corrective lens whether contact lens or spectacle lens in the form of MiyoSmart, we can reduce the likelihood that the child’s myopia will worsen during lockdown.” Essilor Stellest Essilor Stellest lenses are designed to slow down myopia progression in children by creating simultaneous competing defocus. They offer good vision, comfort, and UV protection for all viewing angles. Essilor Stellest lenses slow down myopia progression by 67% on average, compared to single vision lenses, when worn 12 hours a day. Zeiss MyoCare Zeiss MyoCare lenses incorporate cylindrical annular refractive elements (C.A.R.E.) to slow the elongation of the eyeball while delivering good, comfortable vision. They are designed to slow down myopia progression by creating simultaneous competing defocus at the retina.
