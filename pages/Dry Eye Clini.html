Assessment Tools

Tear Break-Up Time(TBUT)

Measures the time it takes for dry spots to appear on the cornea after a blink.

Schirmerâ€™s Test

Assesses tear production by placing a paper strip in the lower eyelid for 5 minutes.

Meibography

Imaging technique used to visualize the structure of the meibomian glands.

Ocular Surface Staining

Uses dyes like fluorescein and lissamine green to detect damage on the surface of the eye.

Treatment Options

We provide the following facilities to ensure comprehensive care for your eyes:

Heat Therapy

Applying warm compresses to the eyelids to unblock meibomian glands and improve oil secretion. Heating Mask for Dry Eye available at Trinity Optics

Artificial Tears

Lubricating eye drops to provide temporary relief from dryness.

Scleral Lens

For severe dry eye patients

Omega-3 Supplements

Dietary supplements to improve the quality of the tear film.

Lid Hygiene

Regular cleaning of the eyelids to remove debris and reduce inflammation.
