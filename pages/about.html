Comprehensive equipments

Our facility is equipped with advanced tools, including an Auto Refractometer, LogMAR and Contrast acuity charts, WelchAllyn/Riester Retinoscope, Keratometer, Slit Lamp with Goldmann Applanation Tonometer, Ishihara's Colour Vision Test Plates, Amsler Grid Chart, Direct and Indirect Ophthalmoscopes, Forus Fundus Camera, and an Advanced Vision Analyzer (AVA) for visual field testing.

Dry eye examination: TBUT, Schirmers test &Meibography

To ensure a thorough evaluation of binocular vision, we also utilize a complete assessment kit comprising Randot Stereoacuity Test, RAF Ruler, Prism Bars, Accommodative Flippers, and Vergence Flippers. Our commitment to technological excellence ensures accurate diagnoses and personalized care for all our patients.
