Mr.Kumaran R

Founder, CEO-Trinity Optics

Founder General Secretary OATN

Adjunct faculty - Elite School of Optometry

R. Kumaran's distinguished career in optometry spans four decades. He began his journey in 1982 as an Optometrist at Christian Fellowship Hospital in Oddanchatram, Dindugal, following his graduation from CMCH Vellore. In 1984, he joined an optical chain, where he served for 17 years, culminating in his role as a Professional Manager. During this period, he mentored numerous Optometrists and sales professionals, many of whom now lead prominent organizations across India.

In 2000, driven by his passion for the field, R. Kumaran established Trinity Optics, Vision Therapy & Contact Lens Clinic in Porur, Chennai. Under his visionary leadership, Trinity expanded its reach, with clinics opening in Keelkatalai (2009) and Kumananchavadi (2011).

R. Kumaran's commitment to education is evident in his role as an Optometry Educator at CSI Kalyani Hospital Allied Health Science College from 2009 to 2018. He currently serves as an Adjunct Faculty at the Elite School of Optometry (Sankara Nethralaya), Chennai.

A respected figure in the optometry community, R. Kumaran is the Founder General Secretary of the Optometric Association of Tamil Nanbargal (OATN). Despite his extensive experience, he remains passionate and actively involved in the operations of Trinity Optics.

Mr. Samuel Livingstone K

Managing Partner & Consultant Optometrist

M.Optom., FVT., FBDO(O/S).,FIACLE

Assistant Professor - Sri Ramachandra Institute of Higher Education & Research

Samuel, a graduate of Sri Ramachandra University (B.Optom, 2007-2011), is a highly accomplished optometrist with a strong focus on patient care and advanced clinical skills. He is one of the few optometrists in India to hold the prestigious Fellowship in British Dispensing Optics (FBDO) from the UK, demonstrating his expertise in providing high-quality and customized eyewear. With a special interest in paediatric dispensing, he ensures optimal visual outcomes for young patients.

Samuel's dedication to patient care extends to vision therapy. He is a Fellow in Vision Therapy (FVT) from Sankara Nethralaya and has established a dedicated Binocular Vision clinic at Trinity Optics. He excels in diagnosing and managing eye muscle and coordination disorders, particularly those related to computer use.

His pursuit of excellence led him to pursue further education, earning a Master's in Optometry from Manipal University (2013-2015), where he was honoured with the "Best Outgoing Student" award. In 2017, he further enhanced his expertise by completing the FIACLE (Fellowship in International Association of Contact Lens Educators).

Currently, Samuel plays a pivotal role as a Managing Partner and Consultant Optometrist at Trinity Optics. He also serves as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Optometry at Sri Ramachandra Institute of Higher Education and Research, actively contributing to the education and training of future generations of optometrists.

Dr.K Sheela Evangeline

R&D and EBP Coordinator

M.Phil(Opt)., FBDO(O/S)., PhD(Australia)

Lecturer, University of New South Wales, Sydney

Dr. Sheela is a highly accomplished optometrist with a strong academic foundation. She completed her Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Optometry at the prestigious Elite School of Optometry, Sankara Nethralaya (BITS Pilani) between 2005 and 2011. A Fellow of the British Dispensing Optics (FBDO), UK, Dr. Sheela possesses in-depth expertise in providing scientifically-driven and precise eyewear solutions.

Following her academic achievements, Dr. Sheela gained valuable clinical experience as a Consultant Optometrist and Lecturer in Optometry from 2011 to 2015. She further expanded her knowledge through rigorous research, earning a PhD in Optometry & Vision Science from Flinders University, Australia.

Currently, Dr. Sheela contributes significantly to the field of optometry as a Lecturer at the University of New South Wales, Sydney, where she imparts her knowledge and expertise to aspiring optometrists. Furthermore, she provides valuable research, scientific, and evidence-based practice support to Trinity Optics.
