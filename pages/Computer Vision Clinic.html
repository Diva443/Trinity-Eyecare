Ergonomics Guidance for Digital Device Use

Our clinic also offers personalized guidance on proper ergonomics to help reduce the risk of CVS and other musculoskeletal issues. Key recommendations include:

Elevate Your Device

Keep your screen at eye level to avoid neck strain.

Take Breaks

Follow the 20-20-20 rule: every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds.

Adjust Lighting

Reduce glare and reflections on your screen.

Use Ergonomic Accessories

Consider using a pop socket, external keyboard, or adjustable chair.

Maintain Good Posture

Sit up straight with your feet flat on the floor.
