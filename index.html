Our Specialized

Trinity Optics stands out as one of the few optometry clinics in India with a team of highly qualified optometrists. Our practitioners hold advanced degrees, including Master's in Optometry, with specializations in:

Paediatric assessment

Paediatric optometry clinic at Trinity Optics specializes in eye care for children, offering services like vision screenings, eye exams, and treatment for eye conditions such as amblyopia (lazy eye), strabismus (crossed eyes), and refractive errors exclusively for myopia management.

Binocular Vision Assessment and Vision Therapy

Addressing binocular vision disorders such as convergence insufficiency, accommodative disorders, and intermittent squints is quite important. Extensive examinations are performed at our clinic.

Scientific Dispensing

Accredited by the Association of British Dispensing Opticians (ABDO), ensuring the highest standards in eyewear selection and fitting. Prescribing prisms to treat vergence anomalies, double vision, and as part of an eye exercise regimen is one of our stand-out practices.
